Classes we offer

Using playful experiences to spark young minds and nurture lifelong learners

Play Way

Designed especially for children aged 2 years and above, our Play Way program welcomes little ones into a cheerful and secure environment. Through music, art, colors, and sensory exploration, we build early motor skills, social interaction, and curiosity—making learning their favorite game.

Nursery

For children around 3 years of age, our Nursery class blends play-based learning with thoughtful routines. With stories, songs, and hands-on activities, we encourage speech development, creative thinking, and a strong emotional foundation. Our nurturing educators ensure every child blossoms at their own pace.

LKG

(Lower Kindergarten)

Our LKG program supports children in the 4+ age group as they begin exploring language, numbers, and basic concepts. We offer a mix of guided learning and imaginative play to develop classroom habits, communication skills, and a love for learning in a collaborative environment.

UKG

(Upper Kindergarten)

For children aged 5 years and above, UKG is the final step before formal schooling. We combine academics with play to enhance reading, writing, and arithmetic skills while encouraging curiosity, self-expression, and teamwork. It's where young learners gain the confidence to soar.
